Look at any modern commercial building  -  from airports to offices, from hotels to shopping centers  -  and you'll notice one thing in common: gypsum is everywhere. Ceilings, interior walls, partitions, decorations  -  all realized with gypsum. But why? What makes this material so essential in modern construction?

As a company that has completed [gypsum works](/en/gypsum-works/) on projects like Vlora International Airport, Green Coast Resort and Lion Park center, we have direct experience with gypsum's advantages at commercial scale.

## 7 Reasons Gypsum Dominates Modern Construction

### 1. Installation Speed
Compared to traditional plastering, gypsum installs up to **5 times faster**. In commercial projects where time is money, this difference is decisive.

### 2. Superior Acoustic Insulation
Double systems with air gap and mineral wool inside achieve up to 60 dB noise reduction  -  essential for offices, hotels and commercial spaces.

### 3. Fire Protection
Gypsum is naturally fire-resistant. Standard gypsum provides 30 minutes resistance; reinforced (FR) gypsum provides up to 120 minutes. In airports and shopping centers, this is a legal requirement.

### 4. Thermal Insulation
Gypsum walls and ceilings contribute to energy efficiency, reducing heating and cooling costs.

### 5. Design Flexibility
No other material offers gypsum's flexibility  -  complex ceiling shapes, curved walls, architectural decorations, and integration of lighting, ventilation and other systems.

### 6. Structural Lightness
Gypsum walls weigh approximately 25 kg/m² compared to 150-200 kg/m² for brick and 250-350 kg/m² for concrete.

### 7. Cost-Effectiveness
Considering materials, labor, time and final quality, gypsum offers the lowest cost per m² of finished surface.

## Gypsum Types for Different Uses

| Type | Application | Characteristics |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Standard (GKB) | Normal walls, ceilings | Standard gypsum core |
| Moisture-resistant (GKBI) | Bathrooms, kitchens | Moisture-treated |
| Fire-resistant (GKF) | Evacuation corridors, kitchens | Special fiber core |
| Acoustic | Studios, conference rooms | High density for insulation |
| Aquapanel | Facades, wet environments | Cement-based, not gypsum |
