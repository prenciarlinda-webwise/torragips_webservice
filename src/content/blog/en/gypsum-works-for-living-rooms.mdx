## Living Room Ceiling Designs: Most Popular Options

### Multi-Level Ceilings

The most requested design for living rooms is the two or three-level ceiling. This style creates visual depth and allows elegant lighting integration. The central level is usually raised while the edges create frames for indirect LED lighting.

For medium-sized living rooms (20-30 m²), we recommend two levels with cove LED lighting. For larger rooms, three levels create a dramatic and impressive effect.

### Indirect LED Lighting Ceilings

Indirect lighting is the number one trend for modern living rooms. LED strips are placed inside ceiling recesses, directing light upward or sideways. The result is a soft, diffused glow that eliminates shadows and creates a relaxing atmosphere.

### Decorative Frames and Profiles

Decorative frames are the detail that makes the difference in any living room. They're placed between the wall and ceiling, creating an elegant transition. Styles range from contemporary minimalist to neo-classical with rich details.

## Decorative Gypsum Wall Features

### 3D Gypsum Wall Panels

Beyond ceilings, decorative gypsum walls are a growing trend for living rooms. 3D gypsum panels, lit niches and wall frames create visual focal points.

### Space Dividers with Gypsum

For open-plan living rooms connected to the kitchen or hallway, decorative gypsum dividers are the perfect solution. They offer visual privacy without blocking natural light.
