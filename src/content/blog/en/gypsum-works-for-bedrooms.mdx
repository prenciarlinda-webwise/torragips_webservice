## Why Gypsum Works for Bedrooms?

Bedrooms have specific needs that make gypsum the ideal material:

- **Acoustic insulation:** Gypsum reduces external noise for peaceful sleep
- **Soft lighting:** Indirect LED integration that doesn't disturb sleep
- **Relaxing design:** Soft shapes and warm colors
- **Moisture control:** Gypsum naturally regulates ambient humidity
- **Thermal insulation:** Helps maintain ideal sleeping temperature

## Bedroom Ceiling Designs

### Tray Ceilings

The most recommended design for bedrooms. The central section rises slightly, creating the illusion of a higher ceiling. Combined with indirect LED lighting, the effect is relaxing and elegant.

### Cove Lighting Ceilings

Cove lighting is ideal for bedrooms as it creates soft, diffused light that promotes relaxation. LED strips are placed inside the ceiling frame, directing light upward.

### Over-Bed Gypsum Panel

A popular new trend creates a decorative gypsum panel on the wall behind the bed extending to the ceiling. This element creates the room's focal point and can integrate indirect lighting and decorative niches.

## Children's Bedroom Gypsum Works

Children's rooms are the perfect opportunity for creativity:

- **Fun ceiling shapes:** Stars, clouds, moon or characters
- **Colored lighting:** RGB LEDs for playful or soothing atmosphere
- **Functional niches:** Spaces for books, toys and decorative objects
- **Good insulation:** Noise reduction for peaceful sleep

## Acoustic Insulation for Bedrooms

If your bedroom is near a road, noisy neighbors or other rooms, acoustic gypsum insulation is a valuable investment, reducing noise by up to 50-60 dB depending on configuration.
