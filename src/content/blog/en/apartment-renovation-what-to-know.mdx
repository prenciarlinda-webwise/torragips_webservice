Apartment renovation is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a homeowner. Done right, it transforms your daily life. Done wrong, it becomes a financial and emotional nightmare. As a team with experience in over 100 renovation and construction projects in Tirana and Durrës, we've seen both inspiring successes and costly mistakes.

This guide helps you avoid pitfalls and plan your renovation with confidence.

## Step 1: Define Your Goal and Budget

Before contacting any team, answer these questions:

- **Cosmetic renovation**  -  painting, new flooring, new accessories
- **Medium renovation**  -  new gypsum ceilings, plastering, modern lighting
- **Full renovation**  -  layout changes, new installations, everything

Set your maximum budget and add 15% contingency. Never start a renovation without a clear budget.

## Step 2: Understand the Work Sequence

The work sequence isn't a matter of preference  -  there's a strict technical logic:

1. **Demolition**  -  removing old walls, tiles, flooring
2. **Electrical installations**  -  new cables, outlets, switches, lighting
3. **Plumbing**  -  water and drainage pipes
4. **[Gypsum works](/en/gypsum-works/)**  -  new ceilings, space dividers, decorative walls
5. **[Plastering](/en/wall-plastering/)**  -  surface preparation for painting
6. **Tiles and flooring**
7. **[Painting](/en/painting/)**  -  final finishing
8. **Doors and accessories**
9. **Furnishing**

## 5 Most Costly Mistakes

1. **Changing plans mid-renovation**  -  every change costs 2-3x more than pre-planning
2. **Choosing team by price alone**  -  evaluate experience, materials, warranty and references
3. **Not planning lighting**  -  points must be decided before gypsum work begins
4. **Cheap hidden installations**  -  low-quality cables and pipes lead to expensive wall-opening repairs
5. **No written contract**  -  always have detailed work lists, prices, deadlines and warranty terms

## Realistic Renovation Timelines

| Type | 60-80m² Apartment | 100-120m² Apartment |
|------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Cosmetic | 1-2 weeks | 2-3 weeks |
| Medium | 3-5 weeks | 5-7 weeks |
